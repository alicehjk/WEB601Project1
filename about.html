About SplashWave

SplashWave is a premier swim training academy offering world-class instruction for swimmers of all levels. Our mission is to promote water safety, build strong techniques, and inspire confidence in and around the water.

  • Founded in 2020 by certified lifeguards and swim coaches
  • Programs available for toddlers to adults
  • Competitive and recreational training paths
